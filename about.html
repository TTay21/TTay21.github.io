About Us

Our Roots

We’ve all learnt that “giant trees grow from small seeds”, so I have always felt the need to help those who had the drive to succeed. Even as I reflect on the days when I overheard my peers complain about the lack of support they received when starting their businesses or witnessing young adults in inner city neighborhoods steer into the wrong direction of life, I knew I wanted to help millennials achieve their goals. Leading with Lee Magazine was created to give aspiring and seasoned young adults a platform to learn, grow, and have a voice.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to connect, empower, and build up boss millenials. We want to help grow their network, boost their reputation and gain knowledge about how to start and grow their own business. We aim to help you so you can help others and help yourself.

Who we are

Leading with Lee Magazine is a black owned media outlet and an award winning digital resource guide for emerging and seasoned entrepreneurs, trendsetters, and game changers. Its where Boss Millennials share brand stories, network, build, and gain knowledge on how to start and grow their own businesses or personal brands. Our readers have 24 hour access to a plethora of business and branding tips ranging from our exclusive guest interviews to our e-Guides. In addition, we cover pop culture, fashion, entertainment, and community News.

The Founder

Lee Whetstone is a Purpose Coach & Strategist, Brand Marketer, Mompreneur, and the Founder and Editor-in-Chief of the award winning publication Leading with Lee Magazine. With an earned Bachelors of Science degree in Business Administration from York College, Ms. Whetstone has decided to use her degree to start a business and personal brand that promote, guide, and uplift millennial entrepreneurs and corporate professionals to live and work in their passion filled purpose. Lee has always had a passion to help others accomplish and develop their gifts. To date, she has helped market numerous of upcoming leaders and small business owners from New York to Nigeria by promoting their businesses and brands as well as developing action steps for those who needed to jump-start and succeed in their careers.
